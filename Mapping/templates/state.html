State Craft BreweryTrends

There are six states that have large Craft Brewery industries, each with over 400 Microberweries, Brewpubs, Taprooms, and Contract Breweries -- California, Colorado, New York, Washington, Pennsylvania, and Michigan. Those states also tend to be among the largest as measured by Craft Barrels produced, although some states such as North Carolina, Florida, and Ohio also rank high by barrels produced.

On a per Capita basis, less populated, rural states such as Maine, Montana, and Vermont, are highly ranked also indicating a strong Craft Brewery culture in those states.
