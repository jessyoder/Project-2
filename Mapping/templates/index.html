National Beer Production Trends -- Total Barrels Produced
National Beer Production -- All Breweries

Over the last 10 years, US beer production has declined from 195MM bbls annually to 179MM bbls annually as consumer tastes have shifted, primarily to wines and spirits.

The large, national breweries, definied as producing 6MM or more barrels annually, have accounted for all of this decline and have lost additional market share to smaller craft breweries producing under 6MM bbls.
